We are Misty Moon Farms!

We grow Local

We currently grow on 2 properties in Denton, County. We have a 15,000 square foot greenhouse where we grow year-round, and 3 acres of field production dedicated to permaculture and biodynamics. We grow fruits, vegetables, and raise ducks for eggs!

We are Fresh

We are a team of young and fresh individuals who are pushing the envelope with how we grow food. We heavily incorporate permaculture, biodynamics, and spirituality into our 3 acre market style garden.

We are Expert

The inspired leader, “Farmer Ross”, has his Master’s in Business Entrepreneurship from Texas A&M and is a Certified Advanced Permaculture Designer. He is continually learning to become a more efficient and ecological farmer.

Allison

Hi I’m Allison and the majority of my interests revolve around being outdoors. I love being active within my environment whether that be skateboarding, biking, kayaking, hiking & backpacking, camping, working out, laying in a hammock while reading a book, or skiing. I’m passionate about gaining new skills/knowledge that I can apply to my life, working with my hands, and growing spiritually. What I love most about farming is that it’s truly fulfilling work and that it gives me an opportunity to connect with the Earth on a deeper level. Nature never ceases to amaze me and it’s inspiring to discover all the different ways we can work with it. In 10 years from now I’d love to have started a business of my own that’s centered around making a positive environmental impact while also encouraging others to live more sustainably. Additionally, I hope that I can touch the lives of the people that I meet in a way that inspires them to expand their consciousness and seek their own spiritual journey.
