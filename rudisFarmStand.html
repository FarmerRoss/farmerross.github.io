Rudi's Fruit Stand!

Rudi’s fruit stand is dedicated to serving the Denton Community April- September. We are open Tuesday through Sunday 10am-6pm. We know folks are nostalgic for those real garden tomatoes and home grown veggies you can’t find at the store. That’s why we sell the best berries, melons, fruits, leafy greens, and duck eggs you can get anywhere around. Our address is 4600 E University Drive, Denton, TX 76201 or find us on google maps.

We also donate HALF of our profits to 501c(3) non-profits in the Local Denton area!
