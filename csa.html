Our CSA

Our CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) is the best way to support local farming and get a great deal on the best tasting, most nutritious foods. Having guaranteed sale of produce helps farmers tremendously.

CSA is a one-time upfront investment that gives weekly produce bags for you and your family.

We do a summer (21 weeks May- September) and winter (12 weeks, October- December) CSA. We have a tentative full menu of what will be included in each CSA bag. Items are subject to change, but we do our best to stick to it.
